Intersting facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleon are reptiles that are part of iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin solor is an important part of communication among chameleon.
  • Most chameleon have prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Intersting facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Intersting facts about Camel

  1. Most camels can survive without food and water for long period of time, as long as weeks.
  2. The eyes of a camel have three eyelids and two rows of eyelashes that prevent sand from entering their eyes.
  3. Camel’s ears are furry, and those hairs keep the sand and dust away from their ears.

Intersting facts about Teddy-bear

  1. The world's smallest stitched teddy bear is a mere 0.29 inches tall.
  2. The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  3. The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.

Intersting facts about Panda

  1. They spend a lot of their day eating.
  2. Their eyes are different to normal bears.
  3. They have great camouflage for their environment.

Intersting facts about Cicada

  1. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  2. Most have red-orange eyes.
  3. The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
